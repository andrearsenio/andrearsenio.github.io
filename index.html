About me

Welcome! I am a researcher and consultant based in Maputo, Mozambique. In my work I aim for integrated approaches for the Urban Water Cycle - bridging sanitation services, water supply and water resources management - as a sustainable way to address water security and environmental pollution, which are serious challenges for populations and decision makers across the world and, in particular, in the Global South. I also believe that a combination of academic research, capacity building at various educational levels and innovative approaches to technology and management, forms a solid foundation to leap-frog development.

From 2009 until 2013 I worked at KWR - Watercycle Research Institute while doing my PhD at the Delft University of Technology, here you can download a copy of my final thesis. Since 2014 I have been involved in several projects throughout Mozambique, to where I moved at the end of 2016.

On this page you can find a sample of the projects where I am currently working on, and if you would like to get in touch, my direct contacts can be found at the bottom of this page. I am looking forward to hearing from you!

My projects

Sustainable water for urbanizing Maputo, Mozambique

Period: 2014-2019
Scope: Research, Capacity building
Partners: TU Delft, IHE Delft, UEM, VEI, FIPAG
Description: NWO-funded research project, through their Urbanizing Deltas of the World program, whereby with the support of four Mozambican PhDs, we have been developing tools, models and approaches that will enable the Mozambican WaSH sector to include water reuse in their water resources plans.
Peer-reviewed publications: Improving health in cities through systems approaches for urban water management (PDF) and Mitigation Potential of Sanitation Infrastructure on Groundwater Contamination by Nitrate in Maputo (PDF)

ZAMADZI, Mozambique

Period: 2017-2021
Scope: Capacity building
Partners: TU Delft, IHE Delft, KIT, WUR, ADZ
Description: ZAMADZI is a NUFFIC-funded capacity building project in Integrated Water Resources Management in the Zambezi Valley. We have four main activities: MSc and PhD projects, Short-duration courses and Action research mini-projects.

VIA Water, Mozambique

Period: 2017-2018
Scope: Innovation, Business development
Clients: VIA Water and Aqua for all
Description: VIA Water supports 60+ innovative water and sanitation projects, and I am the representative for Mozambique where we support 8 projects covering a wide variety of pressing needs, including water, sanitation and urban agriculture.

AIAS Sanitation platform, Mozambique

Period: 2018
Scope: Capacity building, Business development
Clients: BoP InC, AIAS and VEI
Description: I am developing a capacity-building platform to reach 1,000+ people in five years, across the whole country, making use of world-class training materials adapted to the Mozambican context.

Wastewater management and sanitation provision in Africa, Mozambique

Period: 2018
Scope: Advocacy, Knowledge sharing
Client: GRID-Arendal
Description: The Atlas will "profile the state of and trends in wastewater management and sanitation delivery on the African continent" and my task is to produce content for the final document in the shape of case-studies for the city of Maputo.
